Center of the Galaxy
The One is the center of the known galaxy, and it is depicted at the center of every galaxy map. Many people visit The One every year, even from the distant frontiers, in a sort of pilgrimage.
Cradle of Humanity
Dahanno is the home planet of humans (Homo sapiens sapiens), one of the main people in the known galaxy.
Cradle of Elvenkind
Dahanno is the home planet of the Khaldaarim (Homo sapiens khaldaarus), one of the main people in the known galaxy.
